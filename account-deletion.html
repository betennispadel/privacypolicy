Account & Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your Be Tennis & Padel account and associated personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

Please send an email from your registered email address to:

info@btennispadel.com

Include the subject line:

Account Deletion Request

What Happens Next?

  • Your account will be permanently deleted
  • Your personal data will be removed from our systems
  • Requests are processed within 30 days
If you have any questions, contact us at info@btennispadel.com
